Doodles and designs

I really enjoy doodling and creating little designs inspired by astrophysics. You can find some of my art here, you are free to use them as long as you give me credits!

This is a T-shirt design I made during the MPA Kavli summer program in Astrophysics 2023. Each plot represents the project a student worked on during the program, with their names shown above the plot. We printed this design on T-shirts for all the students!
Respect the Fluff
PNG     SVG
This is a drawing I made during the MPA Kavli summer program in Astrophysics 2023. It is inspired by the difficulties of fine-tuning the parameters of the pseudo-vacuum used in grid-based codes, which is sometimes called "fluff".
Fluff Master
PNG     SVG
This is a T-shirt design I made during the MPA Kavli summer program in Astrophysics 2023. It is centered around a plot I obtained with one of my failed simulations, this weird yet amazing shape is due to a combination of factors: wrong pseudo-vacuum parameters (or "fluff"), bad resolution and the cartesian grid of the simulation.
